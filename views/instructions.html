M U L T I - Wordle


Instructions

Guess a five letter word in six attempts.
Submit only valid words by pressing ENTER.
To delete a letter press DELETE.
When the letter is in the chosen word and in the correct position, it will turn Blue.
When the letter is in the chosen word but not in the correct position, it will turn Pink.
Letters that are not in the chosen word will turn Grey.


Multiplayer modes:
Choose to play against 2 opponents.
Whoever guesses in the least amount of tries wins!
OR
Choose a word yourself and have your friends guess the word!
